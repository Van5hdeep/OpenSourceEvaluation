My Mission

UrbanIQ started as a university project fueled by a passion for data and a vision for smarter cities. I saw a gap between the vast amounts of urban data being collected and the ability for everyday citizens to access and understand it.

My mission is to bridge that gap. I believe that by providing clear, real-time insights into traffic, air quality, and weather, I can empower everyone to make better, more informed decisions that lead to healthier and more efficient communities.
