Our Mission

UrbanIQ started as a university project fueled by a passion for data and a vision for smarter cities. We saw a gap between the vast amounts of urban data being collected and the ability for everyday citizens to access and understand it.

Our mission is to bridge that gap. We believe that by providing clear, real-time insights into traffic, air quality, and weather, we can empower everyone to make better, more informed decisions that lead to healthier and more efficient communities.
